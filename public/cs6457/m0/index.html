Overview of Changes

  • Created an alternate attack for the player: a green poison cloud that inflicts 100 damage on all enemies within 5 feet. Usage: X key or Space. Only activates when posion charge is full.
  • Created a poison recharge indicator, which is the the green slider on the bottom right of the HUD. This slider refills over time (5 seconds).
  • The script for the new attack is here: PlayerPoisoning.cs
  • Modified various colors, shaders, and materials to look better / work with unity 5.

Controls

  • Shoot: Left Mouse
  • Poison Cloud: X or Space
  • Movement: W, A, S, D
